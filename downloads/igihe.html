Mu Mateka: Tariki ya 13 Mata

Tariki ya 13 Mata ni umunsi wa 103 mu minsi y’umwaka, hasigaye iminsi igera kuri 263 kugira ngo umwaka ugere ku musozo.

Bimwe mu byaranze iyi tariki

1111: Henry V yimitswe nk’umwami w’abami w’i Roma.

1796: Inzovu nini itigeze iboneka muri Leta Zunze Ubumwe z’amerika,yarahageze iturutse mu Buhinde.

1849: Ubwami bwa Hungary bwahindutse repubulika.

1873: Intambara yabereye muri Louisane” Colfax Massacre “ yaratangiye.

1902: Umushoramari James C. Penney yafunguye amangazini ye ya mbere mu mujyi wa Kemmerer muri Leta ya Wyoming.

1997: Ku myaka 22, Tiger Woods yabaye umukinnyi ukiri muto wa Golf wegukanye igikombe cya amarushanwa y’abagabo ya Golf Masters Tournament.

Bamwe mu bavutse kuri iyi tariki
